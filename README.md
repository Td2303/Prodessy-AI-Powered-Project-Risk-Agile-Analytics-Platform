# Prodessy: AI-Powered Project Risk & Agile Analytics Platform

🏆 Developed for an IIM Indore case challenge on project crisis management and digital transformation.

---

## 📌 Overview

Prodessy is a predictive project-management intelligence platform designed to help organizations identify execution risks, forecast delivery timelines, and optimize resource allocation during high-uncertainty environments.

The solution combines Agile methodologies, Monte Carlo simulations, NLP-driven blocker detection, and machine learning models to provide data-driven recommendations for project recovery and execution excellence.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

A multi-institution educational initiative faced a complete shutdown of in-person collaboration during the COVID-19 pandemic.

Key challenges included:

- 100% loss of physical sprint capacity
- 50% reduction in active contributors
- Delayed accessibility reviews
- Resource and budget constraints
- High stakeholder coordination complexity

The objective was to restore execution capability while maintaining:

- ≥95% content quality
- AODA/WCAG accessibility compliance
- >75% contributor retention
- On-time digital delivery
